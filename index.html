🥇 Williams Method — Metals Focus. Larry Williams' approach uses the Commercial (hedger) COT Index over a strict 26-week lookback as the primary signal, applied here to Gold, Silver, Copper, Platinum, and Palladium. Commercials in metals are producers/refiners hedging supply — when their net position sits near the top of its 26-week range (index ≥90), it means they're covering shorts and expect prices to rise; near the bottom (≤10) means heavy fresh hedging and a bearish read. Between 10 and 90, Williams has no signal — stand aside.
